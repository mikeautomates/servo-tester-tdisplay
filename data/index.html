Continuous Rotation Test

Not running. Tap Start to step through Center → Max → Center → Min → Center.

A standard servo holds position at every step. A continuous-rotation unit spins at MIN/MAX and should stop only at CENTER — if it doesn't fully stop, check for a mechanical trim pot.

⚠ Extended Range

Locked to 900–2100 µs (safe for any servo).

Some servos need pulses outside the safe range to reach their full mechanical travel. Enabling this allows 500–2500 µs. Watch and listen for buzzing, grinding, or stalling — back off immediately if you hear any of that. Resets to locked on every page load / power cycle.
